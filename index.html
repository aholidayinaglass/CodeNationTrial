Equilibrium in Action

Equilibrium is more than a physical state

The meaning of equilibrium is not to be equal, but rather to be settled.


  • Take a break when you feel like nothing is changing

  • If you can't change something, then theres no reason to worry about it

  • Always take your time when making decisions

  • Wear sunscreen ;)
  • Relax
  • Remember to breathe... slowly
  • Send Email

This is best shown in wildlife where different species evolve into an equilibrium even though they are at different stages of the food chain.


In physics entropy can be compared to a state of equilibrium in which all things will end at their most stable state.
